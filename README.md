# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis
### End-to-End Retail Analytics & Business Intelligence Project

---

## Executive Summary

This project delivers a full end-to-end analytics workflow designed to help a retail organization better understand customer purchasing behavior and translate data into strategic decision-making.

Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the project transforms transactional data into business insights that support revenue growth, customer retention, and targeted marketing initiatives.

The repository demonstrates a complete analytics lifecycle aligned with real corporate data workflows.

---

## Business Objective

Retail leadership required answers to key strategic questions:

- What drives higher spending and repeat purchases?
- Which customer segments generate the most revenue?
- How do subscriptions, discounts, and shipping preferences impact sales?
- How can marketing and loyalty programs be optimized using data?

This project was built to provide data-driven answers and actionable recommendations.

---

**Data Volume**

- Customer analytics

## Analytics Workflow

### 1. Data Preparation & Feature Engineering (Python)

Key steps included:
- Data cleaning and validation
- Missing value treatment using statistical imputation
- Standardized schema and column naming
- Feature engineering:
  - Customer age groups
  - Purchase frequency metrics
- Loading clean data into PostgreSQL for business querying

This stage ensured analytics-ready, high-quality data.

---

### 2. Business Intelligence Analysis (SQL)

SQL queries were designed to simulate real corporate analytics requests.

Key business analyses:
- Revenue distribution by gender
- Identification of high-value discount users
- Product performance and customer satisfaction
- Shipping preference impact on spending
- Subscription vs non-subscription revenue comparison
- Customer segmentation (New / Returning / Loyal)
- Revenue contribution by age group

---

### 3. Executive Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to support stakeholder decision-making.

Dashboard capabilities:
- Revenue breakdown by category and demographics
- Subscription performance monitoring
- Customer segmentation insights
- Sales and purchasing behavior trends

---

## Key Business Insights

- Female customers contributed slightly higher overall revenue.
- Express shipping customers spend approximately **12% more per order**.
- Subscription customers represent a **high-value segment**:
  - Significantly higher spending
  - Strong repeat purchase behavior
  - Major revenue contribution
- Loyal customers deliver the highest long-term business value.
- Several products show heavy discount dependency, indicating pricing optimization opportunities.

---

## Strategic Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

1. Expand subscription programs to increase recurring revenue.
2. Introduce structured loyalty initiatives to boost retention.
3. Optimize discount strategy to balance revenue and margins.
4. Focus marketing investment on high-value customer segments.
5. Promote top-rated products in targeted campaigns.
